Origami Instructions And Diagrams


Step by Step diagrams are probably the most popular and easiest way to follow instructions for Origami. Trying to find good origami instructions on the internet can be a lot of work. To help you, we've got some designs for you to try out.

Camel

  • Camel's ears are furry
  • Camel's can move easily across the sand because of its specially designed feet
  • When they find water, they will drink as much as possible

Chameleon

  • A Chameleon's eyes can swivel 180 degrees
  • Chameleons like warm, tropical environments like the rain forest
  • They can change the color of their skin

Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals

Bear

  • Bears are extraordinarily intelligent animals
  • Bears grieve deeply for others
  • Bears have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing

Panda

  • Pandas eat consistently for 12-14 hours a day
  • Adult panda bears can weigh as much as 45 kilos (100 pounds)
  • Newborn pandas are pink

Flying-Cicada

  • Cicadas Live on All Continents Except Antarctica
  • They're Not Locusts
  • They Have One of the Longest Insect Life Spans
